About me:


Hi there! My name is Robert Holloway and I am currently pursuing a Mechanical Engineering Degree from Saint Louis University. While I'm aiming for a rather technical career, my passion is photography, where I'm free to express my creativity and emotional feelings. Travel photography is my #1, but I also enjoy shooting portraits, events, and branching out to cinematography. Below are some of my favorite photos/projects. Let me know what you think!






Travel Photography




Travel photography is my passion, because it involves exploring new places and experiencing new geography, culture, and communities. As you can tell from the photos above, I am partial to the Western US, simply because I love its variation of climate and landscapes. The National Park Service maintains some of the most beautiful ecosystems in the world, and my goal is to someday visit all 58 of them! For my top 5 list of parks, click the emblem below!
